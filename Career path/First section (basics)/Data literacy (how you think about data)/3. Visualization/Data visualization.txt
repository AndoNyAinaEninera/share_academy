Charts can be univariate, bivariate or multivariate (1, 2 or multiple variables)

- Histogram, Linechart, Barchart

- Density curve

- Boxplot, Violinplot

- Scatterplot

- Piechart, Wafflechart, Donutchart

- Univariate map (shows only locations)
- Bivariate or multivariate map (shows geographical map plus additional variable)


Misleading and confusing graphs :
- Axes scaling :
breaks(a revoir topic 2, lesson 2, page 2)
linear scaling
logarithmic scaling

- Color scales :
sequential
diverging
categorical

- Color association (cultural based choice of color)

- Labels, title and annotations
